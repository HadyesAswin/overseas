About Us

Your Education Partner

Since 2007

The one stop solution for all your international study needs

Founded in 2007, Edroots International, is one of the leading study abroad consultancies located in Perinthalmanna, Kerala. With over 17 years of expertise, Edroots has successfully placed more than 18000+ students in top-notch institutions across the world.

At Edroots we provide comprehensive overseas education services assisting students to make the right choice with regard to pursuing higher education in reputed overseas educational institutions. Edroots provides service for higher education in various countries such as the UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, Germany, France, Malaysia, Dubai and has a command over the education system worldwide.

Edroots has always assured premium and reliable services to all our students. We have an array of highly qualified, trained and experienced professionals to guide students to the best possible Institutions, thus enabling them to build their future. We are the stepping-stones to their future and remember that our success is directly linked to their success.
